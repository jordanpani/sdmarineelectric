Ship Profile - ISABELA II- Galapagos Islands

One of the most popular cruising ships in the Galapagos Islands, the M/Y Isabela II conveys the atmosphere of a comfortable and elegant yacht. A seven night cruise on the Isabela II is one of the most luxurious ways of cruising in the Galapagos Islands. Tour the Galapagos with onboard amenities such as a convivial bar/lounge, well appointed, comfortable exterior cabins, plenty of sun deck, and a separate reading and game room. The Isabela II has a seven night cruise from Tuesday to Tuesday and accommodates up to 40 passengers.

Ship Type: Motor Yacht Category: Luxury Class
Ship Length: 166 feet Capacity: 16 Passengers
Ship Width: 38 feet Speed in knots: 16 knots
Equipment: On board gyrocompass, automatic pilot, radar and GPS, fire detector. Fully air conditioned, 110 Volts available, Fax, Environmental Music, Snorkeling Equipment, Life Boats, Life Jackets, Dinghy, Television, Video, Stereo Equipment, House Phone, Medical facilities, Jacuzzi


Crew: There are 24 crew members in addition to 3 bilingual naturalist guides (English, German, Spanish)
 
Cabin Facilities:
21 Outside cabins, with twin or double beds, 110V, air conditioning,amenities, hot and cold Water, house phone, and private bathroom with shower.

Galapagos Cruising is a Year Round Destination
The Galapagos Islands enjoy a mild sub-tropical climate, allowing Galapagos cruise ships to operate on year round. Temperatures are defined almost entirely by ocean currents and the trade winds. In general. From December to May will be warm and sunny; and from June to November will be cool with a breeze in the afternoon.
