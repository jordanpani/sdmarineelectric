Latin America Travel Destinations - Banos, Ecuador

Travel to the South of Ecuador for Three or Four Nights at Luna Runtun

The Resort Offers Spa Treatments and
a Full Range of Adventure Programs -Over Twenty Advenure Programs to Choose From

Driving south from the Quito approximately three hours, through the Avenue of the Volcanoes, enter a magnificent land bordered by two national parks. Located high above the mystical town of Banos, with an eagle eye's view of the town below, the Luna Runtun Resort reigns as one of Ecuador's premier resorts. Nestled in the cloud forest with a view of the national park across the valley, the entrance to the Amazon lies just an hour's drive to the South. With a range of over twenty adventure programs to choose from, each program highlights the splendor of this area from rafting along the river valley, to biking, to hiking among the numerous trails in and out of the forest.....or take a tour to origins of the Amazon basin and hike into see a hidden waterfall and orchids growing wild in the foothills of the Andes.

With over 30 rooms to choose from, deluxe rooms boast stunning views of the city of Banos below in the valley, while standard rooms and family suites take in valley views or capture a mountain view of Tungahurua Volcano... close by and just to the north.

Facilities include a fully functional spa with daily spa treatments; dining room featuring buffet breakfast and lunch and superb dining at night; gift shop; recreation center with pool table, games, and entertainment; swimming pool and hot tubs fed by natural volcanic springs; stables and more.
