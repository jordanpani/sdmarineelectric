Ship Profile - Evolution -Galapagos Islands

This modern ship brings back the elegance and sophistication of a sailing yacht from the era of 1920's with safety and convenience of the twenty-first century. Fully air conditioned, the spacious cabins have either a double or twin bed configuration, with well appointed private bathrooms. In addition, the MV Evolution has a well equipped library and video room. In addition to indoor dining, an al-fresco dining area is available, where you will enjoy the the islands' spectacular scenery over a delicious lunch or barbeque. Passengers love to congregate on the observation deck, where whale or dolphin watching is popular, or just to relax on the comfortable lounge chairs.
Ship Type: Motor Vessel Category: Luxury
Ship Length: 192 feet Capacity: 32 Passengers
Ship Width: 29 feet Speed in knots: 12 knots
Equipment: 11300hp Caterpillar 3512 T1TA Engines, and 150 KVA Northern Lights Generator
Crew: Up to 2 naturalists guides & 1 tour leader plus 18 crew members
Ship Description:The Galapagos Yacht Evoluton was built specifically for cruising in the Galapagos Islands. The luxury class yacht has three levels of cabins. The A cabins are large and cabin A-1 is a suite at 263 squrare feet. There are two other A cabins. The C cabins are on the next deck down and below them are the D cabins. The average size of the cabins on the Evolution is 186 square feet. Most of the cabins are for double occupancy, however, Cabin C-4 is 140 square feet and listed as a single cabin. Bed arrangements vary from double beds to twin beds. There are no bunk beds on the Evolution.
Facilities on Board:
Two bars, a library, a boutique, a small swimming pool, and a saloon. The infirmary includes an MD on ship. There is a, reception area, and dining is both inside and alfresco. Snorkeling equipment, scuba equipment and kayaks may be available. Please request details as the National Park requirements are constantly changing.
Special Children's departures are available. Please call for availability. The Kid's Club offers special adventure programs for children including star gazing and special movie nights. Guides oriented to children are available. The lounge area has playstation and a variety of games for kids of all ages.
Cabin Facilities:
There are 14 cabins available as single, twin, or double cabins. There are also 4 suites. All cabins feature a private a bathroom. air conditioning. Amenities include: hot and cold water, house phone, private bathrooms with shower.

Galapagos Cruising is a Year Round Destination
The Galapagos Islands enjoy a mild sub-tropical climate, allowing Galapagos cruise ships to operate on year round. Temperatures are defined almost entirely by ocean currents and the trade winds. In general. From December to May will be warm and sunny; and from June to November will be cool with a breeze in the afternoon.
