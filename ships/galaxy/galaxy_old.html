Ship Profile - Galaxy - Galapagos Islands

The motor yacht Galaxy - Galapagos based, is a well equipped and spacious motor yacht. Every cabin comes equipped with a full private bathroom, cold and hot water and air conditioner.  Four, five, six, eight, ten, eleven, and fifteen day Galapagos cruise options are available.

Ship Type: Motor Yacht Category: Luxury class
Ship Length: 35 meters Capacity: 16 Passengers
Ship Width: 15.2 meters Optimal Speed: 12 knots
Crew: 9 crew members plus one bilingual naturalist guide
Ship Description: This yacht indeed provides comfortable lounges, a dining salon in addition to common areas with TV and a VCR; well stocked bar, also large viewing windows, and above all, a large and expansive observation deck with sun and shaded areas. The dining salon area and the lounge are very well designed, and this yacht holds a spacious sun deck.  Your cruise in Galapagos Islands will be far more pleasing and lovely in the comfortable and relaxing and intimate environment found on board this yacht. For other yachts that offer Galapagos tours, see the cruise planner page.

Galapagos Cruising is a Year Round Destination
The Galapagos Islands enjoy a mild sub-tropical climate, allowing Galapagos cruise ships to operate on year round. Temperatures are defined almost entirely by ocean currents and the trade winds. In general. From December to May will be warm and sunny; and from June to November will be cool with a breeze in the afternoon.
