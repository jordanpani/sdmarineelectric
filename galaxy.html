Ship Profile - Galaxy - Galapagos Islands

The M / Y  (motor yacht)  Galaxy - Galapagos cruising in the Galapagos Islands, is a well designed, equipped  and spacious 16 passenger motor yacht. Each cabin comes designed  with full private bathroom, hot and cold water and air conditioning.  For your Galapagos itinerary, choose from four, five, six, eight, ten,  and fifteen day Galapagos cruise itineraries.

Ship Type: Motor Yacht M/Y Category: Luxury class
Ship Length: 35 meters Capacity: 16 Passengers
Ship Width: 15 meters Optimal Speed: 12.5  knots
Crew: 8 crew members plus one certified  bilingual naturalist guide
Ship Description: This  Galapagos yacht provides comfortable  spacious lounges, a dining salon as well as common areas including TV and a VCR; well and amply filled bar, with  large viewing windows, and most important,  an expansive, comfortable  observation deck including  sun and shaded areas. The  social areas such as the dining salon area and social lounge are well designed, and the Galaxy yacht offers an ample and  spacious sun deck.  Cruise the Galapagos Islands aboard the comfort and style of the M/Y Galapagos Yacht, GALAXY.  For other yachts that offer Galapagos tours, see the cruise planner page.

Galapagos Cruising is a Year Round Destination
The Galapagos Islands enjoy a mild sub-tropical climate, allowing Galapagos cruise ships to operate on year round. Temperatures are defined almost entirely by ocean currents and the trade winds. In general. From December to May will be warm and sunny; and from July to November will be cool with a breeze in the afternoon.
