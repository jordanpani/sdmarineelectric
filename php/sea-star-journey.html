Sea Star Journey - Galapagos Islands - Ship Profile

The Sea Star Journey cruises in the Galapagos Islands as a deluxe class, 110 foot, motor vessel. It is fully air-conditioned throughout and will accomodate up to 16 passengers in double cabin/staterooms. Each cabin comes equipped with a private bathroom and shower. The well-trained crew and naturalist guide provide excellent service for passengers onboard and offshore in the Galapagos Islands. The Sea Star Journey is great for those who love snorkeling, and family excursions, or hiking, birding, and wildlife viewing, It is an ideal charter yacht for small groups. The Sea Star Journey also offers its guests a very comfortable and spacious yacht with plenty.of deck space.
Ship Type: Motor Yacht Category: Luxury
Ship Length: 110 feet Capacity: 16 Passengers
Ship Width: 23 feet Speed in knots: 12.5 knots
Equipment: The Sea Star Journey comes equipped with 3 generators onboard with a Nissan 110 KW, a Perkins 60 kW and also a Perkins 40 kW capacity providing 110 and 220 A.C. 60 cycles. The Sea Star Journey utilizes marine radios, and also a depth sounder up to 2000 mts.There is onboard a 48 mile radar, and also a G.P.S. Onboard are 2 pangas/ tenders with outboard motors and dbl. ten-man inflatable rubber rafts. .
Crew: The captain, one multilingual naturalist guide, a cook, a machinist, a barman and 4 sailors on hand.
Facilities on Board:
Fully air conditioned with 110V, providing snorkeling equipment, and environmental music onboard. . There are life boats, in addition to raincoats, life jackets, and a dinghy, There is a TV and VHS video system. Onboard is a public restroom, and house phone.
Cabin Facilities:
110V, Air Conditioning, cabin amenities, hot and cold water , private bathroom with shower

Galapagos Cruising is a Year Round Destination
The Galapagos Islands enjoy a mild sub-tropical climate, allowing Galapagos cruise ships to operate on year round. Temperatures are defined almost entirely by ocean currents and the trade winds. In general. From December to May will be warm and sunny; and from June to November will be cool with a breeze in the afternoon.
