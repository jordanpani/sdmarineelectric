Take a one, two or three day guided tour of Mindo

Ecuador - Visit the Mindo Cloudforest with an Overnight at the Arasha Resort

Day 1: Birding in Ecuador - Departing from Quito in the morning from your hotel and drive North West of Quito in the direction of MAQUIPUCUNA - an eco-reserve located in the cloud forest near Mindo. Take a hike in the forest here with your guide to spot the wildlife and the vast variety of birds that occupy this area of Ecuador. After a two hour hike, enjoy lunch at a countryside style inn. Then continue on, driving north through the rain forest, arriving for an afternoon check-in at the resort of Arasha. The resort at Arasha is considered, one of the most beautiful resort spas in South America and winner of international awards.

Day 2: There are a variety of different activities offered in ARASHA such as walks in the forest, bird watching tours, a workshop at the chocolate factory, and visits to the nearby waterfalls. Also included: nocturnal tour of the secondary forest with torches, birding from the mirador, wild orchid guided tour to the River Caoni and much more. Here too is the chocolate factory - an opportunity to learn first hand how chocolate was discovered and manufactured from the local Cacao tree and the fruit it bears. There is also an artisan factory -making earrings, rings, and much more!

Day 3: Departure with your guide and driver from Arasha Resort at approximately 11:00 AM and arrive for lunch at a most unique spot in the cloud forest, Bella Vista -, up in the hills and surrounded by hundreds of hummingbirds. In the late afternoon, return to Quito but not before first taking a stop at the Equator and the Equatorial Monument to visit the most interesting museum ( INTI NAN ), where you will learn a lot about the equator and all the facts applicable to it.
